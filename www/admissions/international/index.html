Coming to Birmingham

In coming to study Computer Science at undergraduate or postgraduate level at the University of Birmingham you are joining a truly vibrant, lively global community, with staff and students from over thirty countries around the world.

As a Russell Group University, and an active member of Universitas 21, our reputation is underpinned by international research of the highest standard, partnered by outstanding learning and teaching delivery.

You can be sure that you will benefit tremendously from the experience of studying in Birmingham. Not only will you secure a well respected computer science degree, which is recognised around the world, but you will also benefit from the friendly multicultural community that exists on our beautiful campus.

By choosing to study at the University of Birmingham you will be joining some 4000 other international students who have decided that Birmingham is the right University for them.

International Visits

Each year, members of staff from the Department and the University undertake a number of international visits, giving you the opportunity to meet us face-to-face and ask any questions you may have about the School, the courses we offer, entry requirements, application process and anything else of interest.

See the schedule of planned trips for the coming year.

Talk to us

We know that coming to a new country to study can be a bit daunting, but we are a really friendly Department, and want to help you settle in as much as we can! We have a great team of UG & PG CS ambassadors who will be happy to answer any questions that you might have.
